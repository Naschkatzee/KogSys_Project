Although training continued for the full 300 epochs, the model maintained extremely high performance throughout the remainder of the run. Training accuracy approached 100%, while validation accuracy consistently remained very close to the optimum, demonstrating excellent generalization capability.

Although the in_out configuration contains hierarchical spatial relationships between inner and outer object groups, these relationships appear sufficiently regular for the architecture to capture effectively. In contrast to the distributed configurations, which require reasoning over larger collections of objects and more complex combinatorial interactions, the symbolic structure of the in_out task appears considerably easier for the model to represent.

Validation performance remained consistently high throughout the training process, providing additional evidence that the modifications required to execute the original repository in a modern software environment preserved the intended behavior of the model.
